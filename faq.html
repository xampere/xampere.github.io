Frequently Asked Questions

We thought you might be having some doubts

Q.1How do I install FlatCharge?
Installing flatcharge is as easy as blowing a whistle. You just need to have inverter lines in your home or workplace and then all you need to do is call a tehnician to drill holes in the wall to hang the FlatCharge and then plug it and it's ready.
Q.2How do I start using Flatcharge?
After you have successfully completed the installation of Flatcharge all you need to do is power it on and setup your smartphone application. You see how easy it is.
Q.3How do I setup the Flatcharge with my smartphone?
You have to download the smartphone application from Google Playstore or Apple Appstore and then just follow the instructions after installing the app.
Q.4What is Flatcharge?
Flatcharge is a smart power backup system for your house and office which is very advanced and cost effective.
Q.5Do we get any warranty for Flatcharge?
Yes, you get 8 years of warranty with the Flatcharge.
Q.6How the Flatcharge is better than the other power backup systems?
Flatcharge is very different than the traditional batteries and inverters. The traditional battery have less energy density and short lifespan whereas the Flatcharge works for atleast 8 years with very advance features.
Q.7What is EPD in FlatCharge?
EPD stands for Emergency POwer Delivery. When you run out of juice in Flatcharge and you are in utter need of power delivery then you can enable the Emergency Power Delivery option on your smartphone to get the limited power in the hour of need. Notice that you can only use this feature for 5 times in a month.
Q.8Do I need to connect any inverter with the Flatcharge?
No, Flatcharge comes with a inbuilt advance inverter which is power efficient, silent and small in size which is perfectly compatible with the Flatcharge.
Q.9What features does the smartphone app has?
The smartphone app had many features like
Estimated Power Usage
Estimated Time to Discharge
Estimated Time to Charge
Battery Health, etc
Q.10Do I need wifi to connect my smartphone to Flatcharge?
No, the Flatcharge connect to your phone using Bluetooth.
Q.11What platforms are compatible with Flatcharge Smartphone app?
The smartphone app is available for both the iOS and the Android OS.
Q.12What happens when Flatcharge is not supplying power?
When Flatcharge is not supplying power then either it must be discharged or the wiring is not done properly.
Q.13Is Flatcharge compatible with Solar Panels, if yes how?
Yes, the Flatcharge is totally compatible with the Solar Panels. You need to buy the Solar Panels on your own and the smart solar module which enables the Flatcharge to properly sync with the Solar Panels.
Q.14Can the Flatcharge provides power supply and charge at the same time?
Yes, Flatcharge can charge from Grid or Solar Energy and at the same time provide power supply to you.
Q.15How does the Flatcharge let me have control of use of Energy?
You have all the control through the smartphone app. You can anytime turn the charging, power delivery on or off with just a touch on your phone.
Q.16Can I purchase only the battery of Flatcharge without inverter and connect it to my previously owned inverter?
No, the Flatcharge is different than the other traditional inverter and batteries and thus it needs different inverter which is specially designed to be used with it.
Q.17What kind of batteries for energy storage does the Flatcharge uses? Are they safe?
The Flatcharge uses the Lithium -ion batteries inside it which are properly attached, insulated and have a cooling system inside. There is very short chance of the short circuit inside it which is about one in a million.
