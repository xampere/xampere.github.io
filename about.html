About XAMPERE

XAMPERE is an started founded in 2017 by a group of college students. We aim to develop an smart home power backup system which will be sleek and stylish unlike the typical inverter and batteries and will be having a life five times as of a typical Lead-Acid battery.

Our Mission

The beautiful looking slim FlatCharge easily sticks to your wall giving your room an immersive look and saving space.
